Our Story

K.T.M. Engineering Consultancy & Construction Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 2019 A.D as an engineering consulting and construction firm specializing in the construction, design, construction supervision and management of civil & Mechanical engineering infrastructure projects such as Hydropower, Irrigation, and Water supply, Pipelines, Buildings and Roads & Bridges etc. KTMECCPL is committed towards providing consulting & construction services of the highest professional standards to its clients. This commitment, coupled with the rich blend of experience, expertise and facilities available at KTMECCPL, makes it one of the most competent & sound firms of Nepal.
