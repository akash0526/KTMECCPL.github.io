Welcome to K.T.M.ECCPL

K.T.M. Engineering Consultancy & Construction Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 2019 A.D as an engineering consulting and construction firm specializing in the construction, design, construction supervision and management of civil & Mechanical engineering infrastructure projects such as Hydropower, Irrigation, and Water supply, Pipelines, Buildings and Roads & Bridges etc. KTMECCPL is committed towards providing consulting & construction services of the highest professional standards to its clients. This commitment, coupled with the rich blend of experience, expertise and facilities available at KTMECCPL, makes it one of the most competent & sound firms of Nepal.

Organizational Details

The company is wholly owned by its principals and associates all of whom are professionals of various fields. The management is thus entirely in the hands of professionals, who have direct and extensive experience of the requirements of the profession. The Directors of the firm and their professional affiliations are shown as below. The day-to-day management of the company is taken care by Chairman, CEO/Managing Director and Technical Director. The working arrangement of the company is such that each assignment undertaken by the company is supervised and managed by one of the directors of the firm, who is designated as the "Chairman/CEO /Director" for that particular project. The Chairman/CEO / Director is selected based on his knowledge and experience in the requirements of the project. The concerned Chairman/CEO / Director will pull together the right team of experts and to ensure technical quality and prompt completion of the project.

Great care is taken in the selection of staff or resource consultants to ensure that the job could be accomplished with delivery of outputs efficiently as per the schedule and by maintaining high professional standards and quality.
